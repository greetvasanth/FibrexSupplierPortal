Registration Highlights


1- Make Sure your information are complete and accurate before submitting the registration.
2- You will be kept informed of the progress of your registration by automatic notification e-mails.
3. Make sure the below documents are available and ready for attachment while registering:
  1. Valid Trade/Commercial License.*
  2. Valid Membership of Chamber of Commerce.*
  3. Signature of the authorized signatory.*
  4. List of major customers/Projects.*
  5. Recent Statement of Work and Labours(for subcontractors).*
  6. Company Profile.
  7. HSE Certificate.
  8. ISO Certificate.
  9. QA/QC Policy.
  10. Insurance Certificate(When Applicable).
  11. Any necessary certificate related to your materials.
* Documents are mandtory.

4- For any assistance on the registration process, kindly send an email to fibrex.procurement@fibrex.ae
5- Please note that registration is our database does not guarantee you any business or tender opportunity from Fibrex.
